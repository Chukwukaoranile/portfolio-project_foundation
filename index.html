Our Mission

At InclusioKids Africa, our mission is to champion the rights and well-being of children with cerebral palsy, one child at a time. We are dedicated to providing access to medical care, care homes, and a strong support system through the power of volunteers and donations. Our goal is to create awareness, educate the public, and establish a compassionate community that advocates for these children and their caregivers. Through collaboration with medical professionals and partnerships with local organizations, we strive to improve the quality of life for abandoned children with cerebral palsy and ensure they receive the love, care, and opportunities they deserve.

Connecting children with cerebral palsy to dedicated volunteers for support, care, and empowerment

Creating awareness. Transforming lives. We strive to educate, challenge stigmas, and build a compassionate community for children with cerebral palsy.

Facilitating meaningful connections between children and dedicated caregivers for compassionate care and unwavering support

Eliminating financial barriers to care. Join us in providing vital support through donations.
