ABOUT US

At InclusioKids Africa, we are driven by a deep passion and commitment to make a positive change in the lives of children living with cerebral palsy in Africa. Witnessing the challenging circumstances they face, including discrimination, lack of caregiving, and financial constraints hindering their access to proper medical attention, we were inspired to take action and create a platform that addresses these critical issues.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform the lives of children with cerebral palsy by creating a supportive community that offers comprehensive care, resources, and opportunities. We envision a world where every child with cerebral palsy can thrive and reach their full potential, regardless of societal barriers.

Our Commitment

As a team, we are committed to the highest standards of integrity, compassion, and professionalism. We prioritize the well-being and best interests of the children we serve, and we are dedicated to creating a safe and nurturing environment for them.

Join us in our mission to make a lasting impact in the lives of children with cerebral palsy. Together, we can create a world where every child is given the love, care, and opportunities they deserve.
